The earth image (earth.png) is originally from the JPL (Jet Propulsion
Laboratory) "Looking at Earth: From 100 miles to 100 million miles" gallery:
https://explorer1.jpl.nasa.gov/galleries/earth-from-space/

The images are released under the "JPL Image Use Policy", which permits use
of the images for many purposes without prior permission (subject to special
cases listed on the website). See
https://www.jpl.nasa.gov/jpl-image-use-policy
for further information.

The image has been edited slightly by adding an alpha mask around the earth,
and then scaled to a lower resolution for in-game use.
